Publication frequency

The journal is publishing three issues in a year. It follows online publication after immediately accepted the article to the editorial team.

Indexing database

We aim to get indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, etc. in the future. The journal is currently indexed in DOAJ, DOI, Crossref, Google Scholar, OpenAlex, Scilit, ResearchBib, Harvard University, USA, etc.

Journal information

The Journal of Arts, Humanities and Social Science (JAHSS) is a peer-reviewed and open-access journal. It aims to expand the thoughts of researchers globally in the fields of Arts, Human Science, and Social Science.
